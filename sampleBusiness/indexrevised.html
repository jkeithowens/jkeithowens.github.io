Company History

Started in 1972 by Ernest W. Owens, and continued by his son Ernest K. and grandsons, Stephen and Keith Owens, our business has long roots in our local community. What began as Ernie's Cleaning Service has grown to a business servicing commercial and rental property management and repair. We are fully bonded and insured, and we are a family business built on family ethics. We also work with local realtors to repair home inspection needs.
